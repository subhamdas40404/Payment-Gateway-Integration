CHRISTIAN ORGANISATION FOR SOCIAL SERVICE


GOAL

Holistic Development of the Society.


VISION

COSS envisages a gender just society in which women, children and marginalized have rights over their own lives.


MISSION

COSS envisages a gender just society in which women, children and marginalized have rights over their own lives and sensitizing, marginalized through education and creating socio economic development.


STRATEGY

  • Formation and Nurturing of Self-Help Groups.
  • Exploring Sustainable Livelihood Portfolio.
  • Participatory Development.
  • Micro Planning.
  • Taking up challenge of RCH/HIV/AIDS.
  • Strengthening Local Self Governance.
  • Capacity Building through organization Seminars, Workshops, Training both Vocational and Non- Vocational and Camps.
  • Research and Documentation.

THRUST AREA

  • Although the organization has not made any limitation in taking up any development activities, however it put more efforts on following issues
  • Women and Child Development.
  • Livelihood Promotion.
  • Capacity Building.
  • Research and Documentation.
  • Health and Hygiene.
  • Reproductive child Health.
  • Local Self Governance.
  • Natural Resource Management.
  • Awareness Generation.
